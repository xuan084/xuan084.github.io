## English

Last October, I had the privilege of presenting our paper, *From Surface to Semantics: Semantic Structure Parsing for Table-Centric Document Analysis*, at the **28th European Conference on Artificial Intelligence (ECAI 2025)** in Bologna, Italy. Standing in front of researchers whose work I had only ever read about in citations was both surreal and humbling.

![Presenting DoTabler at ECAI 2025](/images/2026-04-28-ecai-2025-presentation/1.jpg)
*Presenting DoTabler during the session.*

The three days flew by. ECAI brings together work from across the AI spectrum — from formal logic and reinforcement learning to natural language processing and explainable AI — and the diversity of perspectives was exactly what I had been hungry for as a junior PhD. I sat in on talks far outside my area, exchanged ideas over coffee with people whose problems were nothing like mine, and walked back to my hotel each evening with my notebook fuller than when I left it that morning.

![ECAI 2025 venue](/images/2026-04-28-ecai-2025-presentation/2.jpg)
*The conference venue in Bologna.*

The questions after my talk were sharp but generous. A few left me thinking about ideas in our work from angles I hadn't considered, and one corridor conversation has already turned into a follow-up project I'm excited to share later this year.

![Bologna](/images/2026-04-28-ecai-2025-presentation/3.jpg)
*Bologna's red rooftops between sessions.*

Beyond the science, ECAI was the kind of trip that reminds you research is also a community. The conference banquet had us trading stories across institutions and continents, and I came home with a stack of business cards and a longer list of papers to read than ideas of my own — a quiet reminder, gratefully accepted, of how much there is still to learn.

Thank you to my coauthors and supervisors, to the ECAI organizers, and to everyone whose questions, encouragement, and casual hallway chat shaped this trip into what it was.

## 中文

去年十月，我有幸在意大利博洛尼亚举行的 **第 28 届欧洲人工智能会议（ECAI 2025）** 上，展示了我们的论文 *From Surface to Semantics: Semantic Structure Parsing for Table-Centric Document Analysis*。站在台上面对那些过去只在引文里读到过名字的学者，既有些不真实，也让我倍感谦卑。

![在 ECAI 2025 做报告](/images/2026-04-28-ecai-2025-presentation/1.jpg)
*在分会场介绍 DoTabler 的瞬间。*

三天的会议过得飞快。ECAI 汇聚了 AI 各个方向的工作 —— 从形式逻辑、强化学习，到自然语言处理与可解释 AI —— 这种多元的视角正是我作为博士新生最渴望接触的。我去听了不少远离自己领域的报告，咖啡时间也和与我研究问题完全不同的同行交换想法，每天回到酒店时，笔记本都比早上离开时更厚。

![ECAI 2025 会场](/images/2026-04-28-ecai-2025-presentation/2.jpg)
*博洛尼亚会场一隅。*

报告之后的提问犀利但善意。有几个问题让我从未曾设想的角度重新打量自己的工作；一次走廊里的偶然交流，已经悄悄变成了一个新的合作项目，希望今年内能分享更多。

![博洛尼亚](/images/2026-04-28-ecai-2025-presentation/3.jpg)
*会议间隙的博洛尼亚红屋顶。*

科学之外，ECAI 也再次提醒我，研究本身就是一种社群。会议晚宴上听到的跨机构、跨大陆的故事；最后带回来的是一摞名片，和一份比自己的想法更长的论文待读清单 —— 一个温柔的提醒：还有那么多想学的东西。

谢谢我的合作者和导师，谢谢 ECAI 的组织者，也谢谢每一位用问题、鼓励和走廊上的随手一聊，把这段旅程塑造成它该有的样子的人。
